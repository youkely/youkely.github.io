A short introduction
======
I am an algorithm enginner at Huawei. I received my M.Sc. degree from [ETH Zurich](https://ethz.ch/en.html) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. [Marc Pollefeys](https://people.inf.ethz.ch/pomarc/) at Department of Computer Science. Before that, I received B.Eng. degree from [UM-SJTU Joint Institute (JI)](https://www.ji.sjtu.edu.cn/), [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/).

<b>Research Interests:</b>
* Visual localization and visual SLAM
* Camera calibration and system identification
* State estimation and sensor fusion
* Autonomous driving, robotics, AR/VR
